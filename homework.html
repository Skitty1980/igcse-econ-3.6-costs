Formula Reference Sheet
TC = TFC + TVC
ATC = TC ÷ Q   (or AFC + AVC)
TFC = TC − TVC
AFC = TFC ÷ Q
TVC = TC − TFC  (or AVC × Q)
AVC = TVC ÷ Q
Total Revenue (TR) = Price × Q
Profit = TR − TC
